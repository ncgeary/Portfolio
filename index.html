What Am I About

My name is Nicholas Geary and I am a problem solver. Being able to adapt, is how I've found success. I have experience in a variety of fields including engineering, web development, and manufacturing.

I started my career in manufacturing which led me to earning an associates degree in Computer Aided Design and Drafting from Erie Community College and a bachelor's degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering from the University at Buffalo. These have led me to careers in industries such as textiles, metal fabrication, and healthcare.

Recently I've found myself interested in the world of front end web development and data science. Using my ability to adapt, I am always open to finding new technologies and tools to help me solve the problems infront of me.
